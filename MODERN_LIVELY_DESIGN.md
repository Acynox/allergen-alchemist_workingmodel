### 2. Depth & Dimension
Instead of flat design, we use **layered depth**:
- **Shadows**: Deep, multi-layered shadows (`box-shadow: 0 20px 25px...`) give cards a "lifted" feel.
- **Glows**: Primary buttons have a colored shadow that matches their gradient, creating a "glowing" effect on light backgrounds.

### 3. Typography: Inter
- **Inter** Font Family remains for legibility.
- **Hierarchy**: Bold, heavy headings (700/800 weight) contrasted with clean body text.
- **Letter Spacing**: Tight tracking on large headings for a modern look.
